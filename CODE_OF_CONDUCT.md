# Code of Conduct

KoalaData welcomes constructive participation from people of all backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and patient.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Respect privacy and never publish another person's credentials, identity, private analytics, or correspondence.
- Accept reasonable maintainer decisions about scope, safety, and moderation.
- Help keep issues and pull requests useful to future readers.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, deliberate disruption, impersonation, and disclosure of private information are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `koaladata@koalastuff.net`. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close contributions, restrict participation, or ban accounts when necessary to protect the community. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.

This policy applies in repository discussions and other project spaces controlled by KoalaData maintainers.
